# Third-party notices

This plugin bundles JavaScript ports of third-party stemming algorithms. Each
port's own license is noted in its file under `languages/` and summarized below
(see also the "Licenses & credits" section of the README).

- `languages/ru.js` — Snowball Russian stemmer, BSD license.
- `languages/en.js` — Porter (1980) stemmer, free use.
- `languages/es.js`, `languages/de.js`, `languages/fr.js` — Apache Lucene UniNE
  light stemmers, Apache License 2.0 (https://www.apache.org/licenses/LICENSE-2.0).
